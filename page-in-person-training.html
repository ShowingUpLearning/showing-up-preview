Curiosity Pi

1 day

The very best client-facing teams ask the most thoughtful questions, the kind that prompt clients to say, 'No one has ever asked me that before.' In this highly interactive workshop, your team learn the three forms of curiosity, epistemic, empathetic, and diversive, and how to continuously nurture each one. The result is a noticeable shift in how they see the world, the questions they ask, and how they lay the ground for stronger insights and better ideas.

Solve Complex Problems

1 to 2 days

Your team take on a realistic challenge, helping a major international retailer enter a complex new market. Through the challenge and guided teaching, they develop six practical techniques to think more critically, structure their approach, and solve ambiguous problems with real clarity. They learn to separate signal from noise, frame the right questions, and test their thinking under pressure. The result is a stronger ability to handle complexity and deliver outcomes that meet and exceed client expectations.

Working individually and together, they apply practical creativity techniques to generate ideas grounded in real client contexts. Character and Integrity

1 to 3 days

What distinguishes the most trusted professionals is not just what they do, but how they do it. Acting with integrity, especially when it is not the comfortable choice, is what earns real, lasting trust. This programme builds self-awareness, explores how trust is earned or eroded, and develops a more intentional way of showing up. The result is a team that balances confidence with humility, known as reliable, consistent, and genuinely caring in how they work with others.

Writing and Delivering Compelling Presentations

1 to 2 days

Your team will bring a presentation they are working on, or one that has not yet landed as intended. Working through a 12-step structure, they learn to build authentic presentations that hold any audience's attention and inspire action. The focus is on understanding where people are, crafting a clear and compelling narrative, and taking them forward with confidence. The result is a presentation that does not just inform, but moves people to think differently and act.

Understanding and Uniting Stakeholders: Spiral Dynamics

2 days

People are complex, and so are the systems they create. Understanding what truly drives different behaviours is one of the most valuable skills a client-facing team can develop. Over the past two decades, no model has proven as practical or as revealing as Spiral Dynamics - the model that helped Nelson Mandela to end apartheid. This programme uses it to help your team understand why people think and behave as they do, navigate competing perspectives with confidence, and build the alignment needed to make real progress together.

Consultative Selling

2 days

Consultative selling is the ability to understand a client's world so well that you become part of how they think and solve problems. In this two-day programme, your team learn to ask the questions that matter, listen with genuine care, uncover what really needs to change, and shape solutions that land. The result is a team that clients trust, value, and turn to first, not for what they sell, but for the measurable difference they make.

Winning 85%+ of Request for Proposals (RFPs)

2 days

RFPs demand time, energy, and focused coordination, and the wrong ones can drain all three. One of the surest ways to improve your win rates is to pursue only the opportunities where you have a genuine chance of winning. This programme helps your team decide which RFPs to pursue and respond to them with care, vision, and a compelling connection to what the client actually needs and why you are the right partner to deliver.
