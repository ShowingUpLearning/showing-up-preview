Why it exists

The classroom isn't enough on its own

Most firms train their new starters intensively: a room, a great facilitator, really great material. We've delivered that kind of immersive training for years, and still do. But our clients keep telling us the same thing: the classroom isn't enough on its own.

The moments that decide how someone performs don't happen in the classroom. They happen on a Tuesday afternoon, or at 6pm on a Friday: a tricky stakeholder, a pitch gone sideways, a competitive bid that needs an idea the client has never seen before. You can't always book a workshop for that.
