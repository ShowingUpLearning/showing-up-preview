The Platform

Showing Up is an online learning platform built specifically for consultants, advisors, consultative sellers and internal change makers solving complex problems. It gives your people self-directed, practical development they can access whenever they need it, grounded in our Nine Dimensions framework and shaped by two decades of working with consulting teams worldwide.
Over 80 video modules, 15 self-assessments, and a library of team challenges, with new content added monthly. Every piece of content is mapped to the Nine Dimensions and tagged by career stage, so your people can find the right learning quickly.
Most platforms offer generic professional development. Showing Up was designed from the ground up for consulting, advisory, and client work. Every learning component is built around the real client challenges your team faces every day, and the Nine Dimensions framework ensures development is focused on the specific areas that drive the greatest impact with clients, not just general business skills.
We hear this a lot - and we agree that most video learning is passive and forgettable. That’s why every module on Showing Up is built around application. You watch a short, focused video, then you apply what you’ve learned to your own work, your own clients, your own team. It’s engaging because it’s immediately useful. We also include self-assessments and team challenges that go well beyond watching a screen. See how the platform works.
Modules are bite-sized and practical. Even the longer courses (such as “Running Great Discovery Meetings”) are broken down into 3 to 10-minute learning segments including the core teaching, practical exercises, self-reflection assessments, and team exercises. They are short enough to fit between client meetings, but deep enough to change how someone approaches their next conversation or challenge.
The Nine Dimensions are the values, behaviours and skills that define the very best consultants. We identified them through two decades of research and work with more than 11,000 consultants. They are: Curiosity, Critical Thinking, Creativity, Character, Communication, Collaboration, Curation, Commerciality, and Coaching. Every module, assessment and challenge on the platform maps back to these dimensions. Explore the Nine Dimensions.
